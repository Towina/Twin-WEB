TriBox Website


Website Design

TriBox is a Serbian brand that creates all sorts of premium ECO boxes, specializing in wavy cardboard making.

The task was to create an elegant, modern, and eco looking website using the colors from the logo that would be pleasing to the eye. I enjoyed working on the website and planning the effects and parallaxes so developers has a clearer picture of what I had in mind.

Done for FireFly Solutions in 2018.
