THETKA Fashion


Logo Design / Branding / Patterns /
Marketing / Photo Retouching

Thetka Fashion is a fashion brand my business partner Jelena and I started in Belgrade 2015. Jelena is a seamstress and wanted to start selling unique and hand-made clothes so I joined in with my cotton hoodie ideas and as a designer/marketing person. I did all the branding, marketing, photo-retouching, and custom painting on clothes and buttons that she sewed.

The clothes were unique and one of a kind. Each one was different; hoodie/jackets had wooden hand-painted buttons made specialy for those jackets. Some of the jackets had fabrics from Hobby Lobby that I designed, while others I drew on what was ordered or imagined. The shirts were printed with my illustrations, or I drew on them to test the market and see if it was worth screen-printing them in small series. It was a fun experience and great business even though we no longer work together due to my bussy schedule.

We sold in Mikser House Belgrade, KC Grad, and on some other festivals
