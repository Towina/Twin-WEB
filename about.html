Hi! My name is Ana Baranjin, a person behind TWIN that breathes and lives creativity.

As you might have noticed, all the sections on the homepage say something about my skills as designer through my interests/hobbies. These activities inspire me to create art, appreciate nature, and stay healthy. And I do have a twin sister!

As an educated marketing graduate with years of experience, I strive to know all the little background details before starting on a project, things that don’t make finished products just “pretty and cute,” but functional in relation to the target audiences, goals of the company, and branding image. Staying mindful that the presentation of the project is visible on all the devices and products it will be placed on is equaly important as the design itself.

Great communication is the key to successful projects. As an university educated professional with experience, I try to recommend the best course of action, while keeping in mind the client’s desires and changes they may have, collaborating with the client. It is extremely important to me that the client is satisfied at the end of the journey and I do everything in my power to make the end product competitive in accordance to client’s wishes and industry standards.
