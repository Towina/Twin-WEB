Pocket of Sunshine Farms


Logo / Branding / Packaging

Pocket of Sunshine Farms is a collection of two small urban farms in Ohio. The logo was done under the 99Designs competition which I won 1st place in and continued to work on other items for the brand with a great client behind the Pocket of Sunshine Farms - Halle.

The logo mark was created so it looks like a pocket in the shape of the Ohio state with the small accent on the word shine that brings balance to the whole logo. There is also a regular pocket looking logo-mark that is intended for expansion of the brand to the other states. Halle had amazing feedback and ideas from the beginning which made this an interesting and fun project to work on from the beginning to the end.
