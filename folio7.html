Loli Sticker Pack


Character Design / Illustration

My family used to have a King Charles Cavalier Spaniel dog named Loli who died last summer of an infected tick bite. Everyone was very sad, especially my Mom since Loli was an important family member, not just as a dog, but because of her previous owner.

As someone who uses stickers a lot, my Mom asked me if I could design some King Charles Cavalier dog stickers, so I did. I have tried contacting Viber in hopes that they may accept the sticker pack so we can use it while chatting, but have yet to hear from them. I would love, as I am sure many Cavalier lovers would, to have a sticker pack dedicated only to this breed, and a lovely memory to our Loli.

The stickers were fun to work on through the whole process, sketch and vector.
